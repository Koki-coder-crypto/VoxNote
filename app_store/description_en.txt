VoxNote turns your voice into structured, intelligent notes — instantly.

Whether it's a meeting, a lecture, an idea at 2am, or a quick reminder, VoxNote records, transcribes, and summarizes everything automatically using on-device speech recognition and AI.

── WHAT VOXNOTE DOES ──

🎙️ RECORD
Tap once to record. Pause, resume, or cancel at any time. No limits on the number of recordings.

📝 TRANSCRIBE
Powered by Apple's on-device speech recognition engine, VoxNote converts your audio to accurate text — no audio ever leaves your device.

✨ AI SUMMARY
After every recording, AI automatically generates:
• A clear, concise summary
• Key points extracted from the conversation
• Action items ready to act on

📂 ORGANIZE
Browse all your memos with search. Every memo includes the original audio so you can replay it anytime.

↗️ EXPORT
Share transcripts and summaries via the system share sheet — copy to Notes, send via Messages, or export as text.
